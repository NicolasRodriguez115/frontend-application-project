The Player

Reach player level 50.

Instructions

Experience (XP) can be gained by playing and then the total amount of XP that can be obtained, depends on different factors (see the table below). You can see your player level in the main menu above the Legend - next to your name.

Factor XP Earned
Won match 900 xp
Top 5 Finish 300xp
Time Survived 3xp per second survived 3 XP per second survived (Survival time is based on the squad as a whole, i.e. the lenght of time until the squad wins or is wiped out, or the player leaves the game.)
Kills 50 XP per kill
Damage Done 0.25 XP per 1 damage point
Revive Ally 25 XP per revive
Respawn Ally 200 XP per respawn
First Kill of the Day 500 XP
Killed Champion 500 XP per kill of the champion squad
Kill Leader 50 XP if the player had been the kill leader in a match at any point
Champion Squad 500 XP if the player was the champion squad going into a match
Playing with Friends +5% XP bouns to Time Survived
Playing with Friends (x2) +10% XP bonus to Time Survived
